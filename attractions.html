Yoga & Meditation

We are pleased to announce YOGA classes at Uprising Festival 2020!

Join us at (yoga location) every morning to discover beginner-friendly yoga and meditation practice. Our instructors offer mindful, restorative movements with breath to bring your mind and body to a higher place.

Take a moment each day to slow down, find your center, and revitalize your mind, body and soul before heading back out to the festival! Feel free to bring your own yoga mats, blankets, props or pillows (there will be limited yoga tools available)

Terran's Nature Walk

Attend a nature walk with Terran as she guides you with information about the beautiful earth around you. Realign your soul with the humbling wildlife and plants, collect leaves, search for edible mushrooms, and witness the beauty around you.

Be sure to bring a water bottle, insect repellant, sunscreen, and healthy snacks.
