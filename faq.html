Festival Location / Driving Directions

Our venue is the Lookout Arts Quarry located at 246 Old Hwy 99 N, Bellingham, WA 98229.

Traveling from the North (Bellingham/ BC)

Directions via I-5 South:
Get on I5 South
Follow I5 South to Nulle road
Take exit 242 for Nulle Rd toward S Lake Samish
Turn left onto Nulle Rd
Continue on old Hwy 99 N for .07 miles
Turn right off old Hwy 99 N to arrive at the Lookout arts quarry
** signage on old Hwy 99 N will make the right turn very obvious

Traveling from the South (Seattle/Portland):

Get on I-5 North
Follow I-5 North to Nulle Road
Take exit 242 for Nulle Rd to S Lake Samish
Turn right onto Nulle Rd
Continue on old Hwy 99 N for 0.7 miles

Gate Times

Hours of Operation:

  • Friday, July 17th 12:00pm to 10:00pm
  • Saturday, July 18th 10:00am to 8:00pm
  • Tickets will not be on sale at gate, all online purchase only.

Reentry is not possible. Once you leave, you leave for the remainder of the weekend. Your bracelet will be cut at the gate. Parking or driving will be restricted each day. If you arrive on Saturday, you will be only available for parking and entry during box office hours.

Parking

All parking will begin at 12pm on Friday, July 17th PM, when the Box Office opens. PLEASE do not park on Hwy 99 or your car will be towed.

Our friends at the Quarry will be directing you to where you need to park!

Front Lot parkers will have to haul their gear up to the campground. The Campground has wooded, shaded areas and a grassy area called the Prairie. Cars are not allowed on the Prairie.

We prioritize disability accommodations on the Prairie. Please contact us at contact@subspacemusic.com for disability accommodations.
