Spiritual Family (+Universal Friends) is a volunteer based experience project dedicated to providing a safe space for individuals to express their passions and talents freely. Today we bring you a chance at new beginnings, a place for all to find a home in this world! We are a community of like minded people coming together as one. SFUF is geared towards learning and growing, providing equal opportunities to all. We have, and always will be a grassroots campaign ~ planting the seeds of hope for years to come. We invite you to join us in celebrating life, love, creativity and expression.

The theme for Uprising Festival 2020 is Constellations. Constellations are relatively bright stars that appear to be close to each other in the night sky and for the fifth anniversary, we couldn’t have felt more connected to this theme. We are all connected, we are grouped together to create a large, interesting, talented, and beautiful pattern in the night sky. So, star family, we welcome you to our 5th anniversary as our bright connections group together once again in 2020 to form our constellation.
